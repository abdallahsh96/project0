      How to make a burger:

    1. cut onions
    2. put some oil on heated pan
    3. now apply the beef patty
      • when the patty when gets a golden brown shade:
      • add some onions rings
      • wait for 30 sec
      • remove the patty and onions & apply a slice of cheese while they're still hot
    4. now apply the patty into your bread
      • * you can add some appetizers; Lettuce, Ketchup, Mustard
